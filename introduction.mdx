### Deploy SmartAgents

Once built, deploy your SmartAgents for different use cases:

**Automated Use Cases:**

- Deploy via [API](/api-reference/introduction) for fully automated customer interactions
- Ideal for high trust use cases with deep integrations available.

**Human-Augmented Use Cases:**

- **[Co-Pilot](/smartAgentApp/coPilot)** - AI-suggested responses that agents review and send
- **[Mission Control](/smartAgentApp/missionControl)** - Chrome extension for conversational AI assistance

Both SmartAgent App options provide context-aware AI assistance to enhance agent productivity.
